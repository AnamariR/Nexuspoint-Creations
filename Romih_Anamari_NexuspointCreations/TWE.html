The Wizard's Escape

~ Dive into the magical world of wizards ~

Wizard Exrathel is about 70 years old and is one of the most famous wizards in the kingdom known for his hard work habits and a bit of "crazy" behavior and appearance - he loves magic so much. One day a letter came to him that he had to beautify Prince Lothar for a ball . After Exrathel came to the castle and set out to finish his spell over the prince, a small fly passed in front of his nose making Exhrathel sneeze. The moment he sneezed, the spell went wrong and threw the prince out of his chair, out of the room in front of a crowd of guests. The guests began to laugh out loud at the prince because instead of beautifying him, the spell did just the opposite, it turned him into a fat, decrepit ruler. The spell did not only destroy the prince, Exrathels "Wizard's stone" is broken and without it he can no longer perform complicated magic. He could now only cast ordinary, weak spells that mean nothing to him at the moment because the guards already arrested him. He is thrown into a labyrinth without his wizard's stone, clothes, food, waterâ€¦ Released to rot in the labyrinth.

Will you help and lead him out of the labyrinth?

The Wizard's Escape is an RPG game with a bit of action and adventure feeling. You are taking a role of an old wizard that you have to save from dying in the labyrinth. Your quest is simple, get him out of there. You will encounter various enemies like rats, spiders, skeletons and moreSometimes the enemies will not be your biggest problems. You will have to solve puzzles in order to get out of the room and sometimes if you kill your enemies, you will have problem getting out of the room. It will be tricky but not impossible.

The final boss of the game will be the prince himself. To beat the game you have to beat him.

Are you going to be able to survive?
