As of 15th of 2020 we are a small indie game studio whose goal is to make video games for our beloved gaming community. We are all mostly self-taught. Some of us have programming backgrounds, some have designer knowledge, some have really good organizatinall skills to keep us on track. Founders of this game studio are Anne Marie Abbot and Mark Eldrige.

It all started as a group hobby couple of years back, we all liked to play around some aspects of making video games so we thought about trying to make something together. As time passed by we figured , why not try and make it into something? That is how we decided to make this studio.
